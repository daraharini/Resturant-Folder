A Legacy of Over 15 Years

Grab and Go has been dedicated to honouring the essence of tradition through our food. What started as a small family endeavour has grown into a global culinary destination, known for preserving the true essence of Hyderabadi cuisine.

At Grab and Go, we are not just about serving food; we are about crafting experiences that stay with you. Our world-famous Haleem, recognized and savoured by food lovers worldwide, is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Our success lies in the careful attention to detail in every dish, from our iconic Biryani to our extensive menu of traditional delicacies. Each recipe is a piece of our heritage, passed down through generations and lovingly prepared to ensure that every bite is a journey into the heart of Hyderabadi culinary tradition.



Famous Food Item

The famous food item is Chicken Biryani that is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and generations-old recipes. Our Haleem is a symphony of flavours, blending tender meat with wholesome grains and aromatic spices. Slow-cooked to perfection, each spoonful is a journey through the rich heritage of Hyderabadi cuisine, leaving a lingering taste that beckons you back for more.
